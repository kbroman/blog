support the UW Health nurses

2022-08-25

The UW Health nurses have been seeking recognition of their union. UW Health claims that it cannot legally recognize their union, but Wisconsin Attorney General Josh Kaul issued an opinion that UW Health can voluntarily recognize their union. Yesterday, the nurses voted to hold a 3-day strike, Sept 13-15, 2022. (See the articles in the Wisconsin State Journal and the Milwaukee Journal Sentinel.)

Please join me in supporting the UW Health nurses:

  • Sign up to participate in the picket line, https://tinyurl.com/nursesupportsignup

  • Contribute to their hardship fund, https://bit.ly/funduwnurses

  • Make a public statement of support

  • Write a letter of support to the UW Health board, corporategovernance@uwhealth.org
